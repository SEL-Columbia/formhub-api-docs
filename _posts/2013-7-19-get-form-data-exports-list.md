Depending on chosen format, get:
1. **csv**: CSV Export.
1. **csv.zip**: Zipped CSV Export.
1. **xls**: Excel Export.
1. **kml**: Google Earth KML Export.
1. **zip**: Attachments as ZIP.
1. **gdocs**: URL to an Exported version in Google Docs.
